Frequently Asked Questions

Is this resume builder completely free?
Yes. MakeCV is 100% free — no hidden fees, no watermarks, and no account required. Build and download unlimited resumes.
Do I need to create an account to download my resume?
No sign-up needed. Just fill in your details, pick a template, and click Download — your PDF resume is ready instantly.
What format is the resume exported in?
Your resume is exported as a professionally formatted A4 PDF, ready for printing or digital submission to employers.
Is my resume data stored or shared?
No. Everything stays in your browser. Your personal data is never sent to any server, stored, or shared with third parties.
Which resume templates are available?
MakeCV offers 10 professionally designed templates: Classic, Modern (sidebar), Minimalist, Creative, Executive, Tech/Dark, Bloom, Neon, Aurora, and Swiss — suitable for every industry and career level.
Will my downloaded resume have a watermark?
No. Every resume you download from MakeCV is completely watermark-free. The PDF is clean and professional with no branding overlaid on your content — ever.
Are the resume templates ATS-friendly?
Yes. Our Classic, Minimalist, and Executive templates use clean single-column layouts with standard section headings that are optimized to pass Applicant Tracking System (ATS) screening used by most employers.
Can I use this resume builder on my phone or tablet?
Yes. MakeCV works on all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile. On mobile you can switch between an edit view and a full live preview of your resume with a single tap.
